    Summary of Experiences

    McKenzie is a digital inclusion advocate, adult educator, and applied mathematician located in Indiana. Her work focuses on bridging the digital divide and teaching job-ready skills through community-based action and data-informed programming. She is available for workshops on Data and the Digital Divide, Planning Digital Literacy Training to Transform Your Community, and a variety of Digital Literacy skills from Keyboard and Mouse to Microsoft Office to Intro to Coding.
