    Summary of Experiences

    McKenzie is a digital inclusion advocate, adult educator, and applied mathematician located in Indiana. Her professional work focuses on bridging the digital divide and teaching job-ready skills through community-based action and data-informed programming. She is available for workshops on Data and the Digital Divide, Planning Digital Literacy Training to Transform Your Community, and training sessions on a variety of Digital Literacy skills from Learning Keyboard and Mouse to Microsoft Office to Airtable to Intro to Coding. One of her favorites to offer is Solve a SQL Murder Mystery - combining learning SQL and Relational databases with solving a murder mystery puzzle.
